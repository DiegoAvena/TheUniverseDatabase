"Teegarden's Star c (also known as Teegarden c) is a candidate exoplanet found orbiting in the habitable zone of
Teegarden's Star, an M-type red dwarf star around 12 light years away from the Solar System. It orbits in a
conservative habitable zone around its star. It is fourth closest potentially habitable exoplanet as of July 2019."
